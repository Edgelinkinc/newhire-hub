👋 Welcome, EdgeLink team!

A message from EdgeLink Human Resources & Recruiting

New Hire Hub is your one-stop portal for submitting new candidates and tracking their onboarding progress — every step of the way, from their Magenta Clear email to their first day on the floor. HR manages progress behind the scenes so you always have a clear picture of where your new hire stands.

THE NEW HIRE JOURNEY

1

Submit the candidate

Fill out the Add Candidate form with their info, store, position, and availability. A welcome email goes straight to your new hire.

2

HR handles onboarding

Human Resources tracks every task — ID verification, offer letter, NTID, paperwork, Brinks credentials — and keeps progress updated in real time.

3

Track progress anytime

Check the Candidates tab for live status on every step. You'll get email notifications when tasks complete or need your attention.

4

Day One — they're ready!

Once all steps clear, your new hire is officially ready to start their EdgeLink journey. You'll receive a final confirmation with everything you need.
